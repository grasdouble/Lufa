# Domain-Specific Compliance Requirements

This document defines compliance requirements for high-complexity regulated domains.

## Healthcare Domain

Required sections and compliance considerations:

- **Clinical Requirements section**: Medical workflows, clinical decision support
- **Regulatory Pathway**: FDA clearance/approval requirements (510(k), De Novo, PMA)
- **Safety Measures**: Patient safety protocols, risk mitigation
- **HIPAA Compliance**: Data privacy, security safeguards, PHI handling
- **Patient Safety Considerations**: Adverse event reporting, safety monitoring

## Fintech Domain

Required sections and compliance considerations:

- **Compliance Matrix**: SOC2, PCI-DSS, GDPR, financial regulations
- **Security Architecture**: Encryption, secure transaction processing
- **Audit Requirements**: Audit trails, compliance reporting
- **Fraud Prevention Measures**: Transaction monitoring, risk assessment
- **Financial Transaction Handling**: Settlement, reconciliation, error handling

## GovTech Domain

Required sections and compliance considerations:

- **Accessibility Standards**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, Section 508
- **Procurement Compliance**: Government contracting requirements
- **Security Clearance Requirements**: Clearance levels, authorization
- **Data Residency Requirements**: Data sovereignty, geographic restrictions

## Other Regulated Domains

For domains not listed above with high complexity:

- **Domain-Specific Regulatory Sections**: Industry-specific compliance
- **Compliance Requirements**: Relevant regulations and standards
- **Special Considerations**: Domain-specific safety, security, or privacy needs

## Validation Criteria

For each required section:

1. **Presence**: Is the section present in the PRD?
2. **Adequacy**: Is it documented with sufficient detail?
3. **Gaps**: What specific information is missing?
